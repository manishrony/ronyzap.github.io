About RonyZap

RonyZap is a family-based, women-owned business with decades of experience in the food and beverage packaging industry across the globe. Our journey began with a deep-rooted passion for delivering quality and innovation, honed over years of working with top-tier brands worldwide. Now, we’re on a mission to revolutionize vending by making healthy eating fast, easy, and accessible. Our vending machines are stocked with nutritious snacks and refreshing drinks—perfect for busy days, offices, libraries, schools, gyms, or anywhere life takes you. No junk, just goodness. With our extensive industry expertise, we bring unmatched reliability and a commitment to wellness to every machine we operate.
