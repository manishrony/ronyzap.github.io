About RonyZap

RonyZap is a family-based, women-owned business proudly rooted in Kennett Square, PA. We’re on a mission to revolutionize vending by making healthy eating fast, easy, and accessible. Our vending machines are stocked with nutritious snacks and refreshing drinks—perfect for busy days, offices, libraries, schools, gyms, or anywhere life takes you. No junk, just goodness.
