About Ronyzap LLC

Ronyzap LLC is building a sports‑AI SaaS platform that helps athletes record their performance, get real‑time AI feedback, and track progress over time. Whether you’re training cricket, baseball, soccer, or football, Ronyzap AI turns your camera into a personal coach.

What we’re building

Our platform lets you:

  • Sign up with email or social accounts (Google, Facebook, Microsoft, Yahoo).
  • Pick a sport and record yourself with your device’s camera.
  • Receive real‑time feedback on form, speed, accuracy, and motion.
  • Review detailed analytics and progress charts in your profile.
